Rural Health Transformation Landscape

Research report mapping the probable distribution flow of Federal rural health funds across all 50 states. Data analysis suggests that funding routes primarily follow the established State Office of Rural Health (SORH) infrastructure. This tool predicts whether funds flow through State Departments of Health, Universities, or Independent Boards, assisting in strategic planning and procurement targeting.

Scope: National (50 States) Focus: Funding Distribution Logic Update: Q1 2026 Estimates

Analysis Methodology

Since concrete "Transformation Plans" are not public for every state, this model uses the Primary Grantee Status of the State Office of Rural Health (SORH) as the predictor.

State DOH (78%): Funds managed by Health Dept. High probability of sub-grants or centralized procurement.

University (16%): Funds managed by Academic Centers. Focus on education, data, and technical services.

Other/Ag (6%): Funds managed by Dept of Agriculture or Non-Profits.
