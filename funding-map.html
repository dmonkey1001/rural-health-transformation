Distribution Archetypes

Centralized Command

The State Department of Health retains funds and issues large-scale RFPs for statewide systems.

Dell Strategy: Sell enterprise stacks directly to the State CIO/CTO.

Regional Coalition

The State divides funds among regional non-profits, CINs, or hospital collaboratives.

Dell Strategy: Align with and sell to the lead agency of the regional hub.

Competitive Sub-Grant

The State acts as a pass-through entity, managing a portal for hundreds of small clinic grants.

Dell Strategy: Execute a high-velocity, channel-driven sales approach.

Tribal Sovereignty Shift

Bypassing state bureaucracy in favor of direct, massive allocations to Tribal Nations.

Dell Strategy: Target highly-capitalized Tribal Health Organizations (THOs).
